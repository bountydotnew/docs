## Overview

The Slack integration allows you to create, manage, and track bounties without leaving your workspace. Post bounties in channels, receive notifications, and approve submissionsâ€”all from Slack.

## Prerequisites

- A bounty.new account
- Admin access to your Slack workspace

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add bounty.new to Slack">
    Visit the [bounty.new Slack app](https://bounty.new/integrations/slack) and click **Add to Slack**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize the app">
    Review the permissions and click **Allow** to authorize bounty.new.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your workspace">
    Select the channels where you want to enable bounty creation and notifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating bounties from Slack

Use the `/bounty` command to create a new bounty:

```
/bounty create "Fix login bug" $500
```

### Command options

- `/bounty create [title] [amount]` - Create a new bounty
- `/bounty list` - View all active bounties
- `/bounty status [id]` - Check bounty status
- `/bounty approve [id]` - Approve a submission

## Notifications

You'll receive Slack notifications for:

- New submissions on your bounties
- Status updates on bounties you're working on
- Payment confirmations
- Comments and questions

<Tip>Configure notification preferences in your bounty.new dashboard under Settings > Integrations > Slack.</Tip>

## Managing bounties

### Viewing submissions

When someone submits work for your bounty, you'll receive a message with:
- Submission details
- Quick approve/reject buttons
- Link to view full submission

### Approving work

Click **Approve** in the notification or use:

```
/bounty approve [submission-id]
```

The payout will be processed instantly.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Commands not working">
    Ensure the bounty.new app is added to the channel. Type `/invite @bounty.new` to add it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Not receiving notifications">
    Check your notification settings in the bounty.new dashboard and ensure the app has permission to send messages.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
